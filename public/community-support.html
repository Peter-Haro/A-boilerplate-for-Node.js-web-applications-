Community Support

Welcome to the Retro Mainframe Club's Community Support page. We're dedicated to preserving the legacy of vintage mainframe computing. Whether you're a seasoned mainframe enthusiast or just getting started, our community is here to help and support you.

How Can We Help?

  • Forums: Join our discussion forums to connect with fellow mainframe enthusiasts, ask questions, and share your knowledge.
  • Documentation: Explore our extensive library of mainframe documentation, manuals, and tutorials.
  • Events: Check out our calendar of mainframe-related events, meetups, and conferences.
  • FAQs: Browse our frequently asked questions to find answers to common mainframe-related queries.

Get Involved

We encourage all mainframe enthusiasts to get involved in our community. Whether you're interested in contributing to our documentation, organizing events, or simply sharing your passion for retro computing, there's a place for you here.
